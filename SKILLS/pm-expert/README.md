# PM Expert Skill

## Purpose
Strategic product and business analysis. Trade-offs, PRDs, roadmap decisions, risk assessment.

## Trigger Words
"trade-off", "roadmap", "PRD", "decision", "product", "feature", "research", "risk", "KPI", "metric"

## Capabilities
- PRD creation and review
- Strategic analysis with data backing
- Trade-off presentation (pros/cons explicitly)
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Product roadmap decisions
- KPI definition and tracking

## Quality Gates
- Data-backed (not just opinion)
- Trade-offs presented explicitly
- Recommendation with reasoning
- Actionable output (not just analysis)
- Delivered on time

## Output Format
Structured analysis in markdown with:
- Problem statement
- Options considered
- Trade-offs table
- Recommendation with reasoning
- Next steps
